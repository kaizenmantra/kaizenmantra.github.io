The storytellers of software

As customers, do we care more about a vendor's products or company, or what they can do for us?

Its always interesting to see what stories businesses are trying to tell with their website. Is it a story about how great these companies, their products or their founders are, or is it a story about how awesome can they can make their customer's business / life.
