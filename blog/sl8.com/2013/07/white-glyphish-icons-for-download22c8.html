White Glyphish icons for download

July 13, 2013 Shri

Glyphish

has some excellent free icons for prototyping and just fiddling around. However, the free pack is only available in black color.

I was googling for white ones and came across this excellent post on how to convert black icons into white using imagemagick.

Once I had my dusty old Ubuntu machine fired up, I installed imagemagick and converted this icon set to White icons.

These are posted on github for anyone else who may be interested.

All attributions / credit for this goes to makers of Glyphish.
